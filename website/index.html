About Our Organization

Orion Industria Magna is a specialized resource operations organization in the Star Citizen universe, focused on extracting, processing, and transporting valuable resources throughout known space. Named after the RSI Orion, the pinnacle of mining technology, our organization embodies efficiency, professionalism, and technological advancement.

Founded with the vision of becoming the premier resource operations group in the 'verse, we combine cutting-edge equipment with skilled operators to maximize productivity while maintaining the highest safety standards. Whether mining asteroids in deep space, salvaging derelict vessels, or transporting valuable cargo, our team approaches each mission with precision and expertise.

Our Mission

To extract, process, and transport resources with maximum efficiency while maintaining the highest standards of safety and professionalism.

Our Vision

To become the premier resource operations organization in the Star Citizen universe, known for excellence and innovation.

Our Values

Efficiency, Professionalism, Innovation, Teamwork, and Safety guide all our operations across the stars.

Our Operations

Mining Operations

Our mining division specializes in extracting valuable minerals and resources from asteroids, planetoids, and planetary surfaces throughout the Star Citizen universe. Using state-of-the-art mining ships like the RSI Orion and MISC Prospector, our skilled operators can identify, extract, and process a wide variety of resources.

Mining Capabilities:

  • Asteroid belt mining operations
  • Planetary surface extraction
  • Deep space resource identification
  • Precision extraction of volatile materials
  • On-site ore refinement and processing

Our mining teams are equipped with the latest technology and trained in advanced extraction techniques to maximize yield while minimizing risk. Whether it's common minerals or rare elements, our operators can locate and extract resources with precision and efficiency.

Salvaging Operations

Our salvage division specializes in recovering valuable materials and components from derelict spacecraft, wreckage sites, and abandoned structures. Using specialized salvage vessels equipped with cutting-edge recovery technology, our teams can efficiently extract valuable resources from even the most challenging environments.

Salvaging Capabilities:

  • Spacecraft wreckage recovery
  • Component extraction and preservation
  • Material reclamation from derelict structures
  • Hazardous environment operations
  • Black box and data recovery

Our salvage operators are trained to assess wreckage sites quickly, identify valuable materials, and extract them with minimal waste. This sustainable approach to resource gathering helps recycle valuable materials while clearing space debris.

Transport Operations

Our transport division ensures that extracted resources and salvaged materials reach their destinations safely and efficiently. Using a fleet of dedicated cargo vessels ranging from nimble haulers to massive freighters, we can transport goods across any distance in the Star Citizen universe.

Transport Capabilities:

  • Secure cargo transport across all security zones
  • Specialized handling of volatile and sensitive materials
  • Armed escort for high-value shipments
  • Efficient route planning and fuel optimization
  • Rapid deployment logistics support

Our transport pilots are experts in navigation, evasion, and defensive flying techniques. With our transport services, you can be confident that your valuable cargo will reach its destination intact, regardless of the challenges encountered along the way.

Our Fleet

RSI Orion

The flagship of our mining operations, the RSI Orion is a capital-class mining vessel designed for large-scale resource extraction. With its massive mining laser array and on-board refinery, the Orion can process enormous quantities of ore without needing to return to base.

Role Industrial Mining
Manufacturer Roberts Space Industries
Size Capital
Crew 6-8

The Orion represents the pinnacle of mining technology, capable of breaking down and processing even the largest asteroids. Its advanced sensors can detect valuable mineral deposits from great distances, making it the cornerstone of our resource gathering operations.

Aegis Reclaimer

Our primary salvage vessel, the Aegis Reclaimer is equipped with powerful cutting tools and tractor beams to dismantle and recover materials from derelict ships and structures. Its large cargo hold can store significant amounts of salvaged materials for transport back to processing facilities.

Role Heavy Salvage
Manufacturer Aegis Dynamics
Size Large
Crew 4-5

The Reclaimer's specialized equipment allows it to process salvaged materials on-site, maximizing efficiency and reducing the need for additional transport. Its reinforced hull and defensive capabilities ensure it can operate safely even in hostile environments.

Drake Caterpillar

The backbone of our transport operations, the Drake Caterpillar is a modular cargo ship designed for maximum flexibility. Its detachable command module and configurable cargo sections make it ideal for a variety of transport missions, from bulk resource hauling to specialized cargo delivery.

Role Transport/Cargo
Manufacturer Drake Interplanetary
Size Large
Crew 2-4

The Caterpillar's robust design and defensive capabilities make it well-suited for transport operations in various security environments. Its efficient engines and fuel systems allow for extended range, making it perfect for long-haul cargo missions.
